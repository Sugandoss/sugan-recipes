The Code-Baker, Chef, Tamil Poet and a Mom

Hi there, Welcome to my food blog Chefify. I’m Suganya Geetha Ramadoss, Chef of this website.   My cooking journey started with learning basics at the age of 13. My first recipe (Veg Biryani) was a great hit. I learned a lot by helping the best cook I ever met - my Mom (Geetha) during my college days. Her multi-cuisine recipes were always my source of inspiration and she was famous for the yummy platter she serves back in my home town. It is characterised by the phrase “therukodi varai vaasanai varuthu” (Meaning: Even people at the end of the street can smell the flavour of the food she cooks).
      Post college, my hatred towards hostel food drove me to self-cook. I started cooking my breakfast and lunch. I was oblivious of healthy food and prefer to have lays chips and cake for snacks on a daily basis. After moving to Bangalore post wedding, my hubby and I started exploring various street food centres in Indira Nagar. However, introduction of our angel into our life brought lot of changes to our eating habits. We decided not to eat in hotels and looked for different ways to enhance our home made food..
      During my career break (pretty long one of 8 yrs), I started concentrating more on healthy portions rather than eating junk. We made following changes: - Stopped using maida, sugar, sunflower oils etc., - Replaced store brought items with hand made items e.g. Sambar, rasam powders - Avoided biscuits, cookies and replaced with healthy options..
      It has been an enriching journey post making this change. We strive to lead a healthy life. Being a cook for 2 decades, I am taking this opportunity to share some of the recipes with my family and friends. Hope you will enjoy the same!
